Tip of the week: how to remotely control protection of your devices
It’s hard to secure a home network, which includes various devices like PCs, laptops, tablets and smartphones. One vulnerable gadget puts all of the others at risk. But if Kaspersky Internet Security or Kaspersky Total Security is installed on all of your devices, the problem can be easily solved.
Connect your Kaspersky software to the My Kaspersky portal — and you’ll be able scan every device or the whole home network remotely, as well as update their antivirus databases. Devices of your kids and family members will be fully protected even when you are away, at work or on a business trip.
