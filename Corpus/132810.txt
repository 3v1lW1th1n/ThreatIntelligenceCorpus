Podcast: The Growing Social Media Threat LandscapeFacebook’s Cambridge Analytica scandal in March was just the tip of the social-media iceberg, as malware, hacks and basic privacy concerns continue to increase on social-media platforms.Threatpost’s Lindsey O’Donnell talks with Zack Allen, ZeroFOX’s manager of threat operations, about the threats presented by the broader social-media landscape and the growing issue of malicious content being spread across networks like Twitter, Facebook and LinkedIn.
