Gary McGraw on BSIMM7 and Secure Software Development
Mike Mimoso talks to Cigital CTO and software security pioneer Gary McGraw about the latest results pulled from the Building Security In Maturity Model (BSIMM). The framework measures the secure development activities of some of the world’s largest software companies and enterprises and can be used by other companies to evaluate their development lifecycles.
