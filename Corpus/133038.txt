ThreatList: Top Summer DDoS TrendsOn Tuesday, Akamai released a report on the year’s biggest distributed denial of service (DDoS) attacks. The report illustrates how this time-tested attack method continues to morph and adopt new tricks, and discusses trends to watch as we move into the summer months.According to the study, Summer 2018 State of the Internet/Security: Web Attack, this year has already seen the largest DDoS attack to date while volumes continue to rise, thanks to a bevy of new techniques, such as attackers using memcached servers and other reflector tools.Here is a list of the biggest takeaways from Akamai regarding DDoS attacks for the summer of 2018:(ThreatList is an occasional overview of InfoSec landscape as represented in at-a-glance lists of relevant data.)
