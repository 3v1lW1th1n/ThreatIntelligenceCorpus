
Play with Youtube Videos (You Tube Hacking Part 1)

 
www.keephd.com
KeepHD is a sweet tool that lets you download HD videos off Youtube and other tube sites in to your video editing software! Not only can you download from youtube HD copies of your movies and music, you can also download the mobile 3GP version for your mobile devices plus the standard MP4 and FLV format. Just enter your video URL below, and KeepHD will automatically grab each version of the youtube video clip you want to download.

www.saveyoutube.com
Just write ‘save’ in front of the URL in the address bar and hit enter. For example: www.saveyoutube.com/watch?v=eV7vQ2aqKYw

www.listentoyoutube.com
ListenToYouTube.com is the most convenient online application for converting YouTube flash video to MP3 audio. This service is fast, free, and requires no signup. All you need is a YouTube URL, and our software will transfer the video to our server, extract the MP3, and give you a link to download the audio file.

 
www.splicd.com
