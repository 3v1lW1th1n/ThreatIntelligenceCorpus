LookingGlass Weekly Phishing Activity: May 8, 2017
This week, we saw a decrease in phishing activity for the top 20 brands we’re tracking, grouped by industry. Overall activity decreased >3%. The biggest decrease was in Electronic Payment Systems at >50%, followed by Internet and Information Services at >30% and Telecommunications at >15%.
Increases in phishing activity included: Internet Search & Navigation Services (>65%), Computer Hardware (>20%) and Storage and Systems Management Software (>10%).
Our Phishing Report offers a snapshot into the weekly trends of the top industries that are being targeted by phishing attacks. By pulling information from major ISPs, partners, clients, feeds, and our own proprietary honeypots and web crawlers, we are able to get a 360-degree view of the phishing landscape. The percentages posted on our blog are based on the sum of the phishing threats of the top 20 brands, and do not include anything below the top 20 threshold.
